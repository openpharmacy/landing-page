Digital Scripts, Done Right

OpenPharmacy is your secure digital wallet for Australian electronic prescriptions (eScripts), fully compliant with ADHA standards. Its robust security simplifies storing, accessing, and sharing your scripts with ease. Enjoy seamless prescription management.

Download OpenPharmacy free on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android).

How It Works — 3 Easy Steps to Get Started

  1. Download & Sign Up — Download OpenPharmacy free from Google Play or App Store and sign up in minutes.
  2. Add Your eScripts — Click the eScript link or paste the token sent to your phone or email from your doctor or pharmacy.
  3. Stay in Control — Securely view, manage, and share your eScripts anytime, anywhere — all in one convenient, trusted app.

2 Ways to View Your Prescription

Manual

  1. Receive an eScript token via SMS or email, then go to the Add Script tab.
  2. Enter your eScript token in the app.
  3. Access your prescription details.

Automatic (Active Script List)

  1. Visit Doctor or Pharmacy — Ask your pharmacist to help you create your Active Script List (ASL). They will verify your identity and get your mobile number.
  2. Get ASL Activation Code — After approving the SMS, you'll receive your ASL code (e.g., XYZ-5678) to connect to OpenPharmacy.
  3. Link to Active Script List — Go to the 'Link MySL' tab in OpenPharmacy and enter your activation code. Your full script list will load automatically.
  4. Enjoy Automatic Script Updates — Once linked, your prescription list syncs automatically, showing both eScripts and paper prescriptions.

Frequently Asked Questions

General

How do I create an OpenPharmacy account?

Download OpenPharmacy from the App Store or Google Play, choose your sign-in method, and you're ready to go. No pharmacy codes are required.

Is there a fee to use OpenPharmacy?

OpenPharmacy is entirely free, and you won't encounter any ads while using it.

Which devices are compatible with OpenPharmacy?

OpenPharmacy is available on iOS (iPhone) and Android devices. Check compatibility on the App Store or Google Play.

How secure is my data in OpenPharmacy?

OpenPharmacy uses advanced security measures. Data on your device is encrypted when the app is not in use. Data sent to and from our Australian servers is protected with end-to-end encryption, and server-stored data is also encrypted.

Am I locked into using a specific pharmacy?

No, your prescriptions are yours to fill at any pharmacy you choose.

How does OpenPharmacy connect to Australia's ePrescription system?

OpenPharmacy integrates securely with Australia's National Prescription Delivery Service through the Medication Knowledge platform. This connection ensures compliance with stringent privacy, security, and prescription management standards established by the Australian Digital Health Agency (ADHA) and the Department of Health and Aged Care. We've undergone rigorous audits to confirm that your prescription data is managed safely. You can verify our certification on the ePrescribing conformance register.

Does signing in with Google or Apple share my personal information?

No, using Google or Apple sign-in only shares your name and email address with OpenPharmacy. No other personal details are sent to them.

Scripts

How do I add prescriptions to OpenPharmacy?

You can add prescriptions in several ways: tap the eScript token link sent to you (which opens OpenPharmacy if installed), enter the token manually in the app, or connect to My Script List (MySL) to automatically sync all active prescriptions.

How do I obtain an electronic prescription?

During your next doctor's visit, request an electronic prescription. They'll send it via SMS or email with a link to your eScript token (a QR code). Tapping the link adds it to OpenPharmacy if the app is installed.

How do I get a repeat prescription?

After your pharmacist dispenses your prescription, you'll receive a repeat token via SMS, email, or paper. Request an eScript token and tap the link to add it to OpenPharmacy. Connecting to My Script List (MySL) syncs repeats automatically.

What happens to my prescriptions after they're dispensed?

Once dispensed, prescriptions are moved to an archived list in OpenPharmacy. You can view archived prescriptions in the Archive tab. For repeats, add the new token you receive. Linking to the Active Script List (ASL) automates repeat updates.

Can I view my prescriptions offline?

You can view previously added prescriptions in OpenPharmacy without an internet connection. Adding new prescriptions requires online access.

What if my prescription details are incorrect?

Prescription data comes directly from your doctor, and OpenPharmacy cannot modify it. Contact your doctor or pharmacist to correct any errors.

Account Management

What is the minimum age to use OpenPharmacy?

You must be 18 or older to use OpenPharmacy. Users under 18 require parental or guardian consent.

How can I delete my OpenPharmacy account?

Open OpenPharmacy, navigate to the Profile section (user icon), and select 'Delete Account.' This action is permanent and all data will be lost. If you can't access the app, email hello@openpharmacy.com.
